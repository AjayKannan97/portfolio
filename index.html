About Me

Passionate and ambitious computer science enthusiast with a MS in CS from Arizona State University. Armed with Machine Learning, and Deep Learning to make a meaningful impact in the tech industry.

Bio: Ajay Kannan - A Journey of Resilience and Innovation Introduction: Greetings! I am Ajay Kannan, a computer science enthusiast and recent graduate in MS computer science from Arizona State University (ASU). My passion for technology, combined with my determination to overcome challenges, has led me on a remarkable journey of resilience and innovation. The Early Years: My fascination with computer technology began in my early years. I was drawn to the world of coding and computing, and my interest only grew stronger when I developed a football manager simulator using C++ during my high school project. This experience motivated me to pursue a degree in computer science at the prestigious College of Engineering, Guindy, Anna University. Research and Breakthroughs: During my second year of undergrad, I joined the Solarillion Foundation, where I delved into the captivating fields of Human-Computer Interaction (HCI) and gesture recognition using Machine Learning (ML). As the group leader of ten researchers, we developed a low-cost static gesture recognition system using MEMS accelerometers, which we successfully presented at the GIoT Summit in Switzerland. Continuing our pursuit, I co-led a team that focused on simple dynamic gestures. Together, we created a cost-efficient device and presented our findings at FICC ’18 in Singapore. These research experiences not only honed my technical skills but also ignited my passion for innovation and problem-solving. Overcoming Adversity: An important turning point in my life came when I was selected for an internship at Microsoft. While attending the Global IoT Summit in Geneva to present my paper, I unexpectedly suffered a ruptured aneurysm, leading to a severe brain hemorrhage and right-hemiparesis. This life-altering event taught me the value of resilience and a positive attitude. Despite facing tremendous challenges, I didn't let my health condition deter my dreams. I took a break year from college to focus on rehabilitation, and thanks to the support of the medical team and my determination, I slowly recovered. Microsoft, recognizing my potential, offered me a second internship opportunity, which I wholeheartedly embraced. Mastering the Field: With my indomitable spirit and a burning desire to contribute to the world of technology, I completed my undergraduate degree with the thesis titled, “Food Classification and Ingredients Identification: South-Indian Cuisine.” I then decided to pursue higher studies and joined the MS in Computer Science program at ASU. During my journey at ASU, I persevered despite the challenges posed by the COVID-19 pandemic. In the face of travel restrictions, I remained dedicated to my studies and joined Sigtuple Inc as a data scientist, working on image processing for biological data across India. I returned to ASU in spring 2022 and received the MORE grant to conduct research on few-shot learning for predicting TCR-epitope binding affinity. Inspiration and Empathy: Surviving my life-threatening condition instilled in me a newfound empathy towards others. I realized that technology could be harnessed to help people facing similar challenges. My goal shifted towards developing technologies that would enhance the lives of individuals with disabilities and provide solutions to real-life problems. Future Aspirations: My experiences at ASU and beyond have shaped my vision for the future. I am deeply passionate about the fields of Artificial Intelligence (AI), Machine Learning (ML), and Deep Learning (DL). These cutting-edge technologies have the potential to revolutionize various industries and positively impact the lives of countless individuals. As I move forward, I aspire to delve deeper into the realms of AI, ML, and DL, focusing on their application in creating innovative products and services. I am driven to explore the frontiers of AI-driven research, seeking solutions to complex real-world problems. By harnessing the power of data and algorithms, I hope to contribute to advancements in autonomous systems, natural language processing, computer vision, and more. My goal is to develop AI-driven solutions that promote efficiency, accessibility, and inclusivity. I believe that AI, ML, and DL can empower individuals with disabilities, enabling them to lead more independent lives and participate fully in society. Whether it's through assistive technologies, healthcare innovations, or smart accessibility solutions, I am committed to making a meaningful difference.
